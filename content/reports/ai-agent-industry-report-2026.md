# AI Agent Industry Report 2026

## Executive Summary

The AI agent industry has reached a critical inflection point in 2026. What began as experimental automation has evolved into mission-critical infrastructure for businesses worldwide. This report analyzes key trends, adoption patterns, and future outlook.

## Key Findings

### 1. Specialization Over Generalization
Agents are increasingly specialized for specific verticals rather than being general-purpose assistants.

### 2. India Leading Adoption
Indian SMBs show the highest adoption rate globally at 65%, driven by affordable pricing and WhatsApp integration.

### 3. Multi-Agent Orchestration
Enterprises are deploying 5-15 agents working in concert, up from 1-2 in previous years.
