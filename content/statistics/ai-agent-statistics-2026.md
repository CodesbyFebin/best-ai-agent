## FAQ Section

### What AI agent statistics matter most for Indian buyers?
Indian buyers should track INR pricing availability, GST invoice support, WhatsApp workflows, DPDP readiness, support hours, language coverage, and industry-specific automation outcomes.

### Are AI agent market statistics reliable?
Market statistics are useful when the source, date, sample size, and methodology are clear. Treat unsourced numbers as directional, not procurement evidence.

### Which categories are growing fastest?
Coding agents, voice agents, customer-support agents, no-code builders, and MCP infrastructure are among the fastest-moving categories for Indian teams.

### How should teams use these statistics?
Use statistics to prioritize pilots, budget conversations, and vendor shortlists. Do not use market-size numbers alone to justify a purchase.

### Implementation checklist
1. Define the target workflow, owner, user, input data, and expected output.
2. Estimate monthly cost in INR, including tax treatment and possible overages.
3. Check whether the vendor can provide suitable invoices, procurement terms, and admin controls.
4. Review DPDP Act 2023 implications if personal data is processed.
5. Test English, Hindi, Hinglish, and regional-language examples where relevant.
6. Validate WhatsApp, UPI, Razorpay, CRM, helpdesk, cloud, or database integrations with the exact workflow.
7. Pilot with a small team and compare results against the existing manual process.
8. Document escalation rules, monitoring, rollback steps, and review cadence.
