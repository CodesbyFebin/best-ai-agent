# Corrections Policy

## Commitment to Accuracy
At Best AI Agent, we strive to provide accurate and up-to-date information. However, errors can occur. When they do, we are committed to correcting them promptly and transparently.

## How to Report an Error
If you believe you have found an error in our content, please report it using one of the following methods:
- **Email**: Send a detailed description to corrections@bestaiagent.in
- **Website Form**: Use the "Report Inaccuracy" button available on each review and comparison page
- **Contact Form**: Mention it in our general contact form

Please include as much detail as possible, including:
- The URL of the page containing the error
- A description of the error
- The correct information, if known
- Any supporting sources or references

## Our Review Process
Once we receive a report, our team will:
1. Acknowledge receipt within 24 hours
2. Investigate the claim by checking our sources and verifying the information
3. Determine if a correction is warranted
4. Make the necessary changes to the content
5. Update the "last_updated" timestamp on the page
6. Add a correction notice if appropriate (for significant errors)

## Correction Notices
For significant errors that may have misled our readers, we will publish a correction notice at the top or bottom of the affected page, detailing:
- What was incorrect
- What the correct information is
- When the correction was made

## Timeline
We aim to complete the correction process within 7 business days for most errors. Complex investigations may take longer, and we will keep you informed of our progress.

## Contact Us
If you have any questions about our corrections policy, please contact us at corrections@bestaiagent.in.
