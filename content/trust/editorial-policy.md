# Editorial Policy

Our commitment to independent, transparent, and high-quality AI agent research.

## Independence

All reviews and rankings at BestAIAgent.in are conducted independently. Vendors do not pay for coverage, influence scores, or receive preferential treatment in our editorial content.

**Key principles:**
- No paid placements or sponsored rankings
- Affiliate income does not affect scores
- Editorial team operates independently from commercial interests
- Vendors cannot review or approve content before publication

## Review Process

Each AI agent undergoes a rigorous evaluation process:

1. **Initial testing:** Minimum 40 hours of hands-on testing
2. **Sandbox evaluation:** Controlled environment testing for security and reliability
3. **Feature verification:** All claimed features independently verified
4. **Pricing confirmation:** Pricing verified on vendor websites
5. **Dual review:** Each review reviewed by a second team member
6. **Fact-checking:** Claims verified against official documentation
7. **Editorial review:** Final review by editor-in-chief

## Fact-Checking Standards

Every article is fact-checked before publication:

- Feature claims verified against official documentation
- Pricing verified on vendor websites
- Benchmark scores verified against official leaderboxes
- Claims attributed to specific sources
- Updates logged with changelog

## Conflict of Interest

We maintain strict conflict-of-interest policies:

- **Disclosure:** Team members disclose any financial interests in reviewed companies
- **Recusal:** Conflicted editors are recused from related reviews
- **Transparency:** All potential conflicts disclosed to readers
- **Annual review:** Conflict disclosures reviewed annually

## Content Standards

All content must meet these standards:

- **Accuracy:** All claims verified and sourced
- **Completeness:** Coverage of both strengths and limitations
- **Timeliness:** Information current within 90 days
- **Clarity:** Technical content accessible to non-experts
- **Fairness:** Balanced assessment of all products

## Correction Policy

When errors are found:

1. Corrections published within 5 business days
2. Changelog added to affected page
3. Material errors announced on social media
4. Root cause analysis to prevent recurrence

## Affiliate Disclosure

Some links on BestAIAgent.in are affiliate links. We may earn commissions when you click and make purchases. This supports our independent research and does not influence our rankings or scores.

- Response time: Within 48 business hours
