# Data Deletion Request

## Your Right to Erasure
Under the DPDP Act and GDPR, you have the right to request the deletion of your personal data that we hold about you.

## What Information Can Be Deleted
You can request the deletion of:
- Account information (if you have an account)
- Email addresses and contact information
- Usage data and analytics
- Any other personal data we have collected about you

## What Information Cannot Be Deleted
We may retain certain information if:
- It is necessary for legal compliance
- It is required for legitimate business purposes (e.g., financial records)
- It is anonymized and no longer personally identifiable

## How to Submit a Request
To request the deletion of your personal data, please:
1. Email us at privacy@bestaiagent.in with the subject "Data Deletion Request"
2. Include the following information:
   - Your full name
   - The email address associated with your data
   - Any other information that can help us locate your data
   - A clear statement that you are requesting deletion of your personal data
3. We will verify your identity before processing the request

## What Happens After Your Request
Once we receive and verify your request:
1. We will acknowledge receipt within 24 hours
2. We will locate and delete your personal data from our systems
3. We will confirm the deletion via email within 7 business days
4. We will provide you with a summary of what data was deleted

## Contact Us
If you have any questions about our data deletion process, please contact us at privacy@bestaiagent.in.
