## Quick Answer
Our 42-point scoring system evaluates AI agents across 8 core pillars with special focus on India-specific factors. Scores are calculated from hands-on testing, benchmark results, and real-world usage data.

## Scoring Framework Details

### Core Dimensions

#### 1. Features Depth (15% weight)
- **Unique capabilities** (5 points): Differentiating features, novel workflows
- **Tool integrations** (5 points): API availability, webhook reliability
- **Workflow complexity** (5 points): Multi-step automation, orchestration

#### 2. Pricing Value (15% weight)
- **Cost efficiency** (5 points): Price per feature/usage
- **INR availability** (5 points): Billing in Indian Rupees
- **Free tier quality** (5 points): Generous limits, no credit card

#### 3. India Localization Fit (15% weight)
- **Language support** (5 points): Hindi, Hinglish, regional languages
- **Compliance** (5 points): DPDP Act 2023, RBI guidelines
- **Payment methods** (5 points): UPI, Indian payment gateways

#### 4. Security & Privacy (10% weight)
- **Data handling** (5 points): Encryption, retention, third-party sharing
- **Access controls** (5 points): SSO, RBAC, audit logs

#### 5. Documentation Quality (10% weight)
- **API docs** (5 points): Completeness, examples, SDKs
- **Tutorials** (5 points): India-specific guides, video content

#### 6. Performance Reliability (10% weight)
- **Latency** (5 points): Response times, consistency
- **Uptime** (5 points): SLA, real-world availability

#### 7. Community & Support (8% weight)
- **Community size** (3 points): GitHub stars, Discord activity
- **Support quality** (5 points): Response time, India-local support

#### 8. ROI for India (7% weight)
- **Time savings** (4 points): Hours saved per week
- **Cost reduction** (3 points): Operational efficiency gains

## Score Calculation

### Formula
```
Total Score = Σ(Dimension Score × Weight)

Scale: 1-10 per dimension
Final Score: 0-10 (rounded to 0.1)
```

### Rating Bands
- **9.0-10.0**: Exceptional - Top 5% of tools
- **8.0-8.9**: Excellent - Top 20%
- **7.0-7.9**: Good - Above average
- **6.0-6.9**: Average - Functional
- **Below 6.0**: Not recommended
