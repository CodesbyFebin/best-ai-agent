## Quick Answer
BestAIAgent.in maintains strict editorial independence. We do not accept payment for reviews, test all tools hands-on, and disclose all affiliate relationships. Our recommendations are based on objective testing, not vendor payments.

## Independence Standards

### No Pay-for-Play Reviews
- We do not accept money for positive reviews
- Vendors cannot influence scores or rankings
- All testing uses publicly available plans
- Reviews are written before any vendor contact

### Testing Methodology
- Each tool is purchased/subscribed at retail price
- Tests run on neutral cloud infrastructure
- Multiple authors verify scores independently
- India-specific testing is mandatory for all reviews

### Correction Policy
- Corrections made within 24 hours of discovery
- Major errors trigger full re-testing
- All corrections documented publicly
- Vendors notified of corrections

## Affiliate Disclosure

## Conflict of Interest Handling

### Author Disclosures
Each author must disclose:
- Personal investments in AI companies
- Employment history with vendors
- Speaking/consulting arrangements
- Open source contributions to reviewed tools

### Vendor Relationships
- Vendors can respond to reviews within 7 days
- We publish vendor responses with full context
- No obligation to publish unsolicited vendor content
- Vendors may provide factual corrections only

### Independence Audits
Quarterly reviews by:
- External advisor board
- Community feedback
- Cross-reference scoring
