# AI Agent Reviews

Our expert review team provides in-depth, unbiased evaluations of AI agents based on hands-on testing and our comprehensive 42-point methodology.

## What Makes Our Reviews Different

- **Hands-on Testing**: We actually use the agents in real-world scenarios
- **Objective Criteria**: Based on our transparent 42-point scoring matrix
- **Transparency**: We disclose our testing methods, limitations, and any potential conflicts of interest
- **Regular Updates**: Reviews are updated as agents evolve
- **India-Specific Considerations**: We evaluate how well agents serve Indian users and businesses

## Review Categories

- Coding Agents
- Business AI Agents
- Voice AI Agents
- AI Agent Builders
- MCP-Compatible Agents
- Free and Open Source Agents

## How We Review

Each review follows our standardized process:
1. Feature evaluation and testing
2. Performance benchmarking
3. Usability assessment
4. India-specific factor analysis
5. Pricing and value assessment
6. Editorial fact-checking
7. Final scoring and verdict

## Trust and Transparency

All our reviews include:
- Clear disclosure of testing methods
- Date of last verification
- Affiliate status (if any)
- Fact-checker information
- Changelog of updates
