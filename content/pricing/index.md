For each AI agent, we provide:

## Pricing Models Explained

We categorize agents by their pricing models:
- **Free**: No cost, potentially with limitations
- **Freemium**: Free tier with paid upgrades for advanced features
- **Pay-as-you-go**: Usage-based pricing (per token, per hour, etc.)
- **Paid**: Fixed subscription pricing (monthly/annual)
- **Enterprise**: Custom pricing for organizational needs
- **Open Source**: Free to use and modify, potentially with paid support

## India-Specific Pricing Considerations

We pay special attention to:

- Support for Indian payment methods (UPI, Razorpay, etc.)

- India-specific feature availability
- Localized support and service level agreements

## Price Accuracy Disclaimer

While we strive to keep pricing information accurate and up-to-date, prices can change frequently. We include a "last verified" date on each pricing entry and recommend verifying current prices directly with vendors before making purchasing decisions.
