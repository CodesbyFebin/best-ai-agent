# OpenAI Company Profile

## Overview

OpenAI is an American artificial intelligence research and deployment company founded in 2015. The company's mission is to ensure that artificial general intelligence (AGI) benefits all of humanity.

## Company Structure

### Hybrid Model
- **OpenAI Foundation**: Non-profit arm focused on safety research
- **OpenAI Corporation**: For-profit subsidiary that sells API access

## Products & Services

### Core Products
- **GPT Models**: GPT-3.5, GPT-4, GPT-4 Turbo, GPT-4o
- **ChatGPT**: Conversational AI chatbot
- **API Services**: Embeddings, GPT, DALL-E, Whisper
- **Codex**: Code generation model

### AI Agents (2026)
- **ChatGPT Agent**
- **OpenAI Agents SDK**
- **Code Interpreter** (now advanced)
