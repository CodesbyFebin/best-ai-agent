## Methodology

Our 42-point scoring system evaluates:
1. **Capability** (15%) - Task coverage, model quality
2. **Ease of Use** (15%) - Setup, UI, documentation
3. **Features** (10%) - Integrations, automations
4. **Pricing** (10%) - Transparency, INR estimates
5. **Reliability** (15%) - Uptime, latency
6. **Support** (10%) - Response time
7. **Security** (15%) - Encryption, compliance

## India-Specific Considerations

- **GST Invoice Support**: Required for Indian businesses
- **INR Pricing**: Avoid USD conversion friction
- **Local Support Hours**: IST business hours
- **DPDP Compliance**: Data protection alignment
