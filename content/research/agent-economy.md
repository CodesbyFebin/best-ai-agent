## Quick Answer
The agent economy envisions AI agents as economic participants performing tasks, trading services, and creating value. Agent-to-agent transactions, agent hiring platforms, and automated businesses represent early stages of this economy, with India positioned for multilingual agent services.
 
## What is Agent Economy?
 
Agent economy describes a future where AI agents function as economic actors:
 - Performing paid work autonomously
 - Trading services with other agents
 - Creating and capturing value
 - Participating in markets
 - Owning assets (digital)
 
This extends digital economy concepts to autonomous agents.
