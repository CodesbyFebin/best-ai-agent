## Quick Answer
Digital workforce platforms treat AI agents as employees with roles, performance metrics, and compliance workflows. They manage agent hiring, onboarding, task assignment, and quality monitoring, especially relevant for Indian businesses scaling automation with DPDP and GST compliance.
 
## What is a Digital Workforce Platform?
 
Digital workforce platforms manage AI agents like human employees:
 - **Agent Lifecycle Management**: Hire to retire
 - **Role-Based Task Assignment**: Skills matching
 - **Performance Monitoring**: Quality and cost metrics
 - **Compliance Workflows**: Regulatory requirements
 - **Team Collaboration**: Multi-agent coordination
