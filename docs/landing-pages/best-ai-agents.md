## Our Evaluation Criteria

### 42-Point EEAT Framework
We evaluate each AI agent across 9 dimensions:
1. **Features** (15%) - Capability depth and breadth
2. **Pricing** (15%) - Cost and value for Indian budgets
3. **India Fit** (15%) - Regional language and compliance
4. **Security** (10%) - Data protection and encryption
5. **Compliance** (5%) - DPDP, GDPR, and Indian regulations
6. **Documentation** (10%) - Quality and completeness
7. **Performance** (10%) - Speed, accuracy, reliability
8. **Community** (5%) - Support and ecosystem strength
9. **Support** (3%) - Responsiveness and availability
10. **ROI** (2%) - Business value and time savings

### India-Specific Factors
- **INR Pricing**: All costs shown in Indian Rupees
- **DPDP Compliance**: Data protection act adherence
- **Data Residency**: AWS Mumbai and GCP Delhi options
- **Regional Languages**: Hindi, Hinglish, Tamil, Telugu, etc.
- **Payment Methods**: UPI, Razorpay, Cashfree compatibility
- **Cloud Infrastructure**: Indian data center recommendations

- Learning curve

- Cost per month

### For Startups (1-50 employees)
Evaluate based on:
- Time to deploy
- Integration capabilities
- India-specific support
- Cost-effectiveness
- Scalability
